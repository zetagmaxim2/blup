01 · Fruity & Tropical
Sunset Lagoon
Tropical Bliss
Golden blend of mango and pineapple brightened by lemon, finished with coconut cream for a dreamy island escape.
Highball
cubed ice
🧊Cubed Ice
🍹Highball Glass
⏱~2 min
💪Easy
Ingredients
40mlMango mixMix
40mlPineapple juiceJuice
15mlLemon juiceJuice
20mlCoconut mixMix
10mlVanilla syrupSyrup
🍍 Garnish
Pineapple wedge on rim · toasted coconut rim · paper umbrella
Method
1
Rim the glass
Run a pineapple wedge around the rim of a highball glass, then dip into toasted coconut flakes. Set aside.
2
Fill glass with ice
Add a generous amount of cubed ice to your prepared highball glass.
3
Build the shaker
Add mango mix, pineapple juice, lemon juice, coconut mix, and vanilla syrup into your cocktail shaker.
4
Add ice & shake
Fill shaker with cubed ice. Close and shake vigorously for 12–15 seconds until the outside feels frosty cold.
5
Strain & pour
Strain through a Hawthorne strainer into your prepared glass over the ice.
6
Garnish & serve
Add a pineapple wedge on the rim and a paper umbrella. Serve immediately.
Pro tip: For extra creaminess, add an extra 10ml of coconut mix. Shake harder — the aeration makes it silkier.
02 · Fruity & Tropical
Passion Storm
Exotic Intensity
Passion fruit meets orange in a vibrant encounter lifted by grenadine sinking like a tropical sunset.
Hurricane
crushed ice
🧊Crushed Ice
🌀Hurricane Glass
⏱~3 min
💪Easy
Ingredients
50mlPassion fruit mixMix
40mlOrange juiceJuice
10mlGrenadineSyrup
10mlLemon syrupSyrup
🍊 Garnish
Orange half-wheel · maraschino cherry on pick · mint sprig
Method
1
Fill glass
Pack a hurricane glass tightly with crushed ice.
2
Shake
Add passion fruit mix, orange juice, and lemon syrup into the shaker with ice. Shake hard for 12 seconds.
3
Strain
Strain the mixture directly over the crushed ice in the hurricane glass.
4
Sink the grenadine
Pour grenadine slowly over the back of a bar spoon so it sinks to the bottom, creating a sunset layer. Do not stir.
5
Garnish & serve
Add an orange half-wheel, cherry on a pick, and a fresh mint sprig. Serve immediately before layers blend.
Pro tip: The grenadine sink only works if you pour it very slowly and the drink is cold. For a more dramatic effect, chill the glass in the freezer first.

06 · Berry & Floral
Berry Velvet
Deep & Lush
Raspberry and blackberry syrups meld with cranberry in a jewel-toned shake finished with vanilla.
Coupe
no ice
❌No Ice — Chilled
🍸Coupe Glass
⏱~3 min
⭐Intermediate
Ingredients
20mlRaspberry syrupSyrup
15mlBlackberry syrupSyrup
50mlCranberry mixMix
10mlVanilla syrupSyrup
15mlLemon juiceJuice
🫐 Garnish
3 blackberries on pick · sugared rim · micro mint leaf
Method
1
Chill the coupe
Place the coupe glass in the freezer for at least 5 minutes, or fill it with ice water while you prepare the drink.
2
Sugar rim
Discard ice water from the glass. Wet the rim with lemon juice and dip into fine white sugar.
3
Build shaker
Add all ingredients — raspberry syrup, blackberry syrup, cranberry mix, vanilla syrup, and lemon juice — into the shaker.
4
Shake
Fill shaker with ice and shake hard for 15 seconds. The goal is maximum chill.
5
Double strain
Strain through both a Hawthorne strainer AND a fine mesh strainer into the chilled coupe. This creates a perfectly smooth, seed-free result.
6
Garnish
Skewer 3 blackberries on a cocktail pick and lay across the coupe. Add a single micro mint leaf.
Pro tip: Double straining is essential here — it gives the drink its velvety, ultra-smooth texture that earns the name.

Blueberry Haze
Mellow & Sweet
Blueberry swirled into cranberry and apple — hazy, subtly sweet, beautifully hued.
Rocks Glass
crushed ice
🧊Crushed Ice
🥃Rocks Glass
⏱~3 min
⭐Intermediate
Ingredients
25mlBlueberry syrupSyrup
40mlRaspberry mixMix
30mlApple juiceJuice
15mlLemon juiceJuice
5mlSugar syrupSyrup
🫐 Garnish
Blueberry skewer · lemon twist · edible viola flower
Method
1
Pack with crushed ice
Fill a rocks glass tightly with crushed ice.
2
Shake
Combine blueberry syrup, raspberry mix, apple juice, lemon juice, and sugar syrup in the shaker with ice. Shake hard for 12–15 seconds.
3
Double strain
Strain through Hawthorne and fine mesh strainers over the crushed ice to create the characteristic hazy texture.

5
Garnish
Thread fresh blueberries onto a pick and rest on top. Place an edible viola flower for colour contrast.
Pro tip: The crushed ice with double strain creates a beautiful "hazy" effect in the glass. Don't skip either step.
09 · Berry & Floral
Raspberry Reverie
Tart & Dreamy
Raspberry double and cranberry with lemon — tart enough to wake you, sweet enough to dream.
Coupe
no ice
❄️Chilled Coupe
🍸Coupe Glass
⏱~3 min
⭐Intermediate
Ingredients
50mlRaspberry mixMix
20mlRaspberry syrupSyrup
30mlCranberry mixMix
15mlLemon juiceJuice
🌷 Garnish
Dehydrated raspberry · rose petal · lemon zest spiral
Method
1
Chill the coupe
Fill coupe with ice water for 3–5 minutes while preparing the drink. Discard when ready to pour.
2
Shake
Add raspberry mix, raspberry syrup, cranberry mix, and lemon juice to the shaker. Fill with ice and shake vigorously for 15 seconds.
3
Double strain
Strain through both strainers into the chilled coupe glass. The surface should be perfectly smooth.
4
Lemon spiral
Use a channel knife or peeler to make a long lemon spiral. Drape it inside the coupe so it curls elegantly.
5
Garnish
Place one dehydrated raspberry and a single rose petal on the surface. Serve immediately.
Pro tip: The balance between raspberry's tartness and the cranberry's body is key. Taste before serving and adjust lemon to preference.

16 · Creamy & Indulgent
Coco Velvet
Silky & Rich
Coconut mix and milk shaken hard with vanilla — smooth, opaque, a tropical dessert in a glass.
Rocks Glass
ice sphere
🔮Ice Sphere
🥃Rocks Glass
⏱~3 min
⭐Intermediate
Ingredients
50mlCoconut mixMix
40mlMilkDairy
15mlVanilla syrupSyrup
10mlPineapple syrupSyrup
🥥 Garnish
Toasted coconut flakes on top · vanilla pod swizzle · pineapple chip
Method
1
Place ice sphere
Set a large ice sphere or cube in a rocks glass.
2
Shake very hard
Add coconut mix, milk, vanilla syrup, and pineapple syrup to the shaker. Add ice and shake VERY hard for 18–20 seconds — the aeration is what makes it silky.
3
Fine strain
Strain through a fine mesh strainer over the ice sphere. The result should be creamy and opaque.
4
Garnish
Scatter toasted coconut flakes on top. Add a vanilla pod as a swizzle stick. Lean a dehydrated pineapple chip against the ice.
Pro tip: Milk-based drinks must be shaken harder and longer than others. The vigorous shake creates a micro-foam that gives Coco Velvet its characteristic smooth body.

Golden Hour
Warm & Radiant
Orange and mango with apricot and energy drink — liquid gold that glows from within.
Highball
cubed ice
🧊Cubed Ice
🍹Highball Glass
⏱~2 min
💪Easy
Ingredients
50mlOrange juiceJuice
30mlMango mixMix
15mlApricot syrupSyrup
10mlLemon juiceJuice
50mlEnergy drink (top)Soda
✨ Garnish
Gold sugar rim · orange twist · dehydrated mango chip
Method
1
Gold sugar rim
Wet the rim with orange juice and dip into gold sugar. Fill highball with cubed ice.
2
Shake the base
Add orange juice, mango mix, apricot syrup, and lemon juice to the shaker with ice. Shake for 12 seconds.
3
Strain over ice
Strain into the prepared highball glass over ice. The warm amber-gold colour is beautiful at this stage.
4
Top with energy drink
Gently pour chilled energy drink down the inside of the glass to preserve fizz.
5
Garnish
Express and twist a wide strip of orange peel over the drink, then perch on the rim. Add a dehydrated mango chip for texture and colour.
Pro tip: The apricot syrup is the secret depth here — it adds a warm stone-fruit note that makes the golden colour feel edible. Don't skip it.
